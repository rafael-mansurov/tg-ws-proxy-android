# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want this project to stay useful, calm, and easy to work with.

Please:

- be respectful
- discuss code, not people
- explain disagreements with facts
- avoid harassment, insults, or baiting
- assume good intent until there is a clear reason not to

## Unacceptable behavior

This includes:

- personal attacks
- harassment or threats
- hate speech
- trolling or deliberate disruption
- publishing private information without permission
- repeated bad-faith arguments after maintainers ask to stop

## Maintainer responsibility

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that break these rules.

## Scope

This applies in issues, pull requests, discussions, and other project communication spaces.
